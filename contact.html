Get in touch

Questions, feedback, bug reports, or feature ideas — all welcome. Tracenil is a small, independent project, and hearing from the people who use it genuinely shapes where it goes.

Bug reports & feature requests

Since Tracenil is open source, the best place for technical issues or feature suggestions is our GitHub issue tracker: github.com/tracenil/tracenil/issues. It keeps everything in the open, where anyone can follow along.

A note on privacy

If you email us, we'll only see what you choose to send. We don't use a tracking system or a marketing list — your message is just an email, used only to answer you.
